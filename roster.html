Rationale

tech house - bass house - futuristic broken beat robot funk - classic house

Daniel Hengeveld aka Rationale produces bassline-driven dance music with a playful underground feel inspired by airport terminals and late nights in his home city of Los Angeles. Behind the decks he tends toward textured tech house and futuristic broken beat robot funk, and is known for his exuberant DJ booth dancing. His latest record Believe It came out November 3 on Motivator.

Soundcloud - Mixcloud - Twitter - Facebook

dpeshMo

bass house - future house - chill bass music

With a musical background in classical cello and Swedish pop music, Mo Kudeki is well-versed in dance floor curation. Her mixes range from floor-filling house to more laid-back magical chillout tunes.

DJ Spacecamp

tech house - trap - future breaks - anything with a sax solo

Perhaps best-known for his well-loved podcast A State of Trap, DJ Spacecamp is perhaps Zero Fucks' most flexible DJ, playing a wide variety of formats in San Francisco regularly. He's also an editor and regular contributior to industry publication DJ Techtools.

Cielux

future house - future bass - bangers - trap - breaks

Named after an advanced color-changing light, Cielux's DJ performances mirror his namesake. Spinning sets that create energetic dancefloors, Cielux moves through stylish house tracks into bass heavy bangers and synth forward future trap. He's in the process of putting together the first Zero Fucks podcast mix series.

Soundcloud - Mixcloud - Twitter
