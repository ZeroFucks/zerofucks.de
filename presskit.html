Zero Fucks

Over the last year, House of Bass, the monthly house night from San Francisco's Zero Fucks crew has regularly hit capacity at the intimate loft at Public Works, perhaps the city's most reliable venue for top shelf international house and techno events.

This success can largely be attributed to the signature House of Bass sound, which nicely balances modern deep and tech house, garage-influenced festival tunes, and the current San Francisco sound of lighthearted and bass-heavy house music.

The second key element of the continuing success of House of Bass is the friendly, unpretentious attitde of the Zero Fucks crew. This is not one of those parties where everyone is very serious about making sure everyone is having fun in exactly the right way, it's a casual affair where everyone is part of the family, and a DJ booth full of dancing strangers at the end of the night is the rule rather than a reason to call security.
